Our Story

Tie‑Dye Unity — Peace Through Color

Founded in 2022, Tie‑Dye Unity began as a neighborhood gathering where friends shared vibrant dye‑dipped art. Today we host workshops, sell hand‑blended textiles, and foster community projects that celebrate cultural diversity and mutual respect.
